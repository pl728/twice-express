Jihyo - Biography

Jihyo, born on February 1, 1997, is a South Korean singer and the leader of the popular girl group TWICE. She began her journey in the entertainment industry at a young age and made her debut as a member of TWICE in 2015.

Jihyo's birth name is Park Ji-hyo, but she is commonly known by her stage name. Her strong vocal abilities, captivating stage presence, and exceptional leadership skills have contributed to TWICE's success.

Jihyo has played a pivotal role in TWICE's achievements, guiding the group with her charisma and dedication. Her powerful vocals can be heard in many of TWICE's popular songs, including "Like OOH-AHH," "TT," "Feel Special," and "Cry For Me."

Alongside her music career, Jihyo has showcased her versatility through various solo and collaborative projects. Her talent, hard work, and genuine personality have earned her a devoted fan base, known as "Once."

Jihyo's leadership and love for her fellow members have solidified her role as the backbone of TWICE. She continues to inspire fans with her perseverance, determination, and ability to overcome challenges.

As TWICE continues to flourish, Jihyo's contributions as a leader and performer remain integral to the group's success. Fans eagerly anticipate her future endeavors and the growth of both Jihyo and TWICE as artists.
