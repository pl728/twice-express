Tzuyu - Biography

Tzuyu, born on June 14, 1999, is a Taiwanese singer and member of the South Korean girl group TWICE. She gained immense popularity after appearing on the reality survival show "Sixteen" in 2015, which led to her debut as a member of TWICE.

Tzuyu's full name is Chou Tzuyu, and she is often recognized for her stunning visuals, tall height, and graceful dancing skills. Her captivating beauty and charming personality have garnered her a large fan base both in Korea and internationally.

As part of TWICE, Tzuyu has contributed to numerous successful albums, including hits like "Cheer Up," "TT," "Likey," and "Fancy." She has also participated in various variety shows and endorsements, further establishing herself as a popular idol.

Despite her young age, Tzuyu's talent, hard work, and dedication have made her one of the most influential and admired idols in the K-pop industry. Her fans appreciate her humble nature, warm smile, and strong work ethic.

Tzuyu continues to shine as a performer, captivating audiences with her performances on stage and bringing joy to her fans worldwide. Her journey in the world of entertainment is still ongoing, and her fans eagerly anticipate her future endeavors.
